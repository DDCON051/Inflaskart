***MANUAL***

Inflaskart is a web application demo that allows you to virtually shop your groceries online.

All the available virtual stores are located in San Francisco, CA, in the following zip code areas:


94102, 94103, 94104, 94105, 94107, 94108, 94109, 94110, 94111, 94112, 94114, 94115, 94116, 94117, 94118, 94121, 94122, 94123, 94124, 94127, 94129, 94131, 94132, 94133, 94134, 94158.


This map of the city may be helpful (source: cccarto.com).


Delivery is free for any order over $20 (depending on each store's delivery area). There's a $3 delivery fee for orders under $20.

There's a 10% service fee for your personal shopper.

If you want to purchase an item in stock in a store that doesn't deliver your address, you can place your order and pick it up at the store location within 24hrs after checking out.
